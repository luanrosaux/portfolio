SaaS Platform · Internal Product

Designing 360 Performance Review

Turning routine evaluations into a clearer, more useful growth system for a team of 100+ professionals.

Role
UI/UX Designer
Timeline
Jan 2024 – Apr 2024
Sector
SaaS / HR Tech
Focus
Research, forms and review workflows

context

At Object Edge, the review process needed to support admins, employees and mentors with different goals while increasing engagement and reducing operational friction.

The challenge

The product had to serve three user groups with distinct needs: admins managing data, employees completing reviews and mentors interpreting results for development conversations.

product visuals

Selected screens showing research inputs, reporting, filtering, review forms and responsive behavior.

process

How the experience was shaped.

01

Research and Personas

Interviewed users, reviewed competitors and mapped the needs of admins, regular users and mentors.

02

Evaluation Experience

Designed intuitive evaluation forms with clear section demarcation, comments and progress feedback.

03

Operational Features

Added CSV upload, review statuses, result overviews and shortcuts to reduce manual work and improve navigation.

impact

What changed after the redesign.

100+
Professionals impacted by the platform.
50%
Estimated review time reduction.
Smoother
Feedback experience reported by team members.
