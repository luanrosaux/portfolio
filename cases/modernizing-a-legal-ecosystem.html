Legacy Product Redesign

Modernizing a Legal Ecosystem

Transforming a dated legal research tool into a focused workspace for search, reading and account management.

Role
Lead UX/UI Designer
Timeline
3 months
Sector
LegalTech
Focus
Search UX, onboarding and design system

context

Magister needed to compete with modern legal platforms by reducing legacy friction, improving search efficiency and giving legal professionals a more comfortable reading environment.

The challenge

A cluttered legacy interface made the product harder to use, increased support requests and weakened confidence among legal professionals.

product visuals

A broader view of the redesigned legal ecosystem: onboarding, search, filtering, security and account-management flows.

process

How the experience was shaped.

01

Frictionless Access

Redesigned login, registration and recovery flows to reduce cognitive load and communicate trust.

02

Intelligent Search Architecture

Moved the product toward a results-oriented interface with collapsible side filters and clearer feedback states.

03

Technical Reading Experience

Improved hierarchy, spacing and typography for long sessions with dense legal documents.

impact

What changed after the redesign.

40%
Faster search for legal research tasks.
-30%
Reduction in support tickets.
25%
Increase in user retention after the redesign.
