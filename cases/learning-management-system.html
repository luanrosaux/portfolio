EdTech · Learning Platform

Learning Management System

A customizable LMS designed to help companies offer education benefits and increase employee engagement with learning.

Role
Lead UX/UI Designer
Timeline
September 2022 – December 2022
Sector
EdTech / B2B SaaS
Focus
Mobile-first LMS, dashboards and company management

context

Creating a flexible learning experience for companies that want to develop their employees.

The challenge

Companies needed a customizable learning platform with a strong course library, clear management tools and a mobile-first experience that could increase employee engagement with training and development.

product visuals

Selected screens from the LMS, from admin dashboards to learner-facing flows.

process

How the product experience was shaped.

01

Research and benchmarking

Used previous research, competitor analysis and existing business-unit guidelines to shape a more consistent LMS experience.

02

Mobile-first design

Prioritized mobile usage while adapting the experience for desktop with additional management capabilities.

03

Testing and refinement

Validated flows through usability testing and refined navigation, layout and management actions based on feedback.

impact

Designed to support scalable corporate learning.
